Identification of Eye Witness Tweets

Identifying Eyewitness during Disaster Identifying Eyewitness  is an important area of research in cognitive psychology and human memory. Eyewitnesses frequently play a vital role in uncovering the truth about a disaster. The evidence they provide can be critical in identifying, charging, and ultimately saving the stucked people. That is why it is absolutely essential that eyewitness evidence be accurate and reliable and twitter is one such source.
